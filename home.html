Administration Faculty

  • President/Rector: Leads strategic goals, oversees academic and legal compliance, and represents the university in public engagements. Ensures quality and guides educational direction.
  • Chancellor: Responsible for university administration and finance, including budget management, resource allocation, and oversight of administrative staff.
  • Dean: Heads faculties, manages academic programs and staff, ensures curriculum standards, and coordinates departmental development.
  • Account Officer: Manages all financial records, handles fee collection, salary disbursal, purchases, and financial audits.
  • Finance Officer: Oversees budget planning, expenditure approvals, fund allocations, and financial reporting for the university[web:20][web:26][web:27].
  • Scholarship Officer: Administers student scholarships, processes applications, maintains records, and coordinates fund disbursement for eligible students.
  • Student Administration Manager: Manages admissions, student records, course registrations, and provides support for student services and grievance redressal[web:19][web:21][web:26].
  • Human Resources Manager: Handles staff recruitment, workplace policies, payroll, and supports faculty development.
  • IT Manager: Maintains university IT systems, supports educational technology infrastructure and campus networks.
