About University of Rajabaran

I am glad that I have been given an opportunity to serve as the Chancellaer of University of Rajabaran -the oldest one in the University. It is an honour and privilege for me to lead this prestigious institute which has a rich history of its distinction in teaching, research and service to the nation. whose foundation stone has been laid by the former prime minister, Rambaran in the year 2003. Over a period of more than two decades, UoR has emerged as one of the leading institutions in engineering and technology. Warangal is the second largest city of the state of Uttar pradesh being the first one which has several places of interest to visit. Some of the cultural heritages worth seeing in . University of Rajabaran is poised to create an ambience for nurturing innovation, incubation, creativity and excellence within its faculty and students towards achieving the sustainable development goals. We emphasize on student centric education with interdisciplinar y research and development for the benefit of Industry and Society.University of Rajabaran becomes a preferred institution for top rankers in Joint Entrance Examination (Main) because of its excellent teaching learning process, infrastructure that a student needs for effective learning. Our alumni have received distinguished recognitions in their chosen areas of profession and extend support towards sustainable development of the institute. I invite participation of all the stakeholders in the growth plan of the institute to steer it to the next level of success in all fronts.
